Home Page

Welcome to the Leaf Me Alone company data management system. This platform can help you update your customer records, orders, plant stock, and suppliers. See below for a quick overview of what actions you can perform for each record.

Customers

Within the "Customers" page, you are able to view the current customers of Leaf Me Alone. You will also be able to add new customers, edit customer information and delete customer records.

Orders

The "Orders" page allows you to view all the orders for Leaf Me Alone. You can add new orders, update existing orders, and delete orders.

Plants

The "Plants" page provides an inventory of plants Leaf Me Alone currently sells. View this page to update the inventory or make adjustments, add new plants to the inventory, or delete a plant from our stock.

Suppliers

Next, our "Suppliers" page gives you a view of all of our plant suppliers. Navigate to this page to update, add, or delete suppliers for our plants.

Plants Suppliers

Finally, the "Plants Suppliers page allows you to see which plants are supplied by a provider.
